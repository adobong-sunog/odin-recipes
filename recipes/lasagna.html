Lasagna





Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian-American comfort food, loved for its layers of rich flavors and creamy textures. This hearty dish consists of alternating layers of pasta sheets, savory meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese, all baked to perfection. The meat sauce is usually made with ground beef, tomatoes, and a mix of herbs and spices, while the béchamel sauce provides a luscious, creamy contrast. The final result is a deliciously satisfying meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 24 oz marinara sauce
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add onions and garlic, sauté until translucent.
  4. Add ground beef to the skillet and cook until browned. Season with salt, pepper, basil, and oregano.
  5. Pour marinara sauce into the skillet and simmer for 10 minutes.
  6. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese until well combined.
  7. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  8. Place three lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  9. Spread one-third of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  10. Sprinkle one-third of the mozzarella cheese over the ricotta layer.
  11. Repeat the layers two more times, ending with a final layer of mozzarella cheese.
  12. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  13. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes or until the top is golden and bubbly.
  14. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.
